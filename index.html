How it works

1. Upload a photo

Drag in a photo or drop it from your phone. We process EXIF securely in your browser.

2. Auto‑extract GPS

If EXIF GPS exists, we place a precise pin. No GPS? You can still add a note or try another photo.

3. Deploy your pin coin

Create a beautiful card with photo, coordinates, and ticker.

4. Explore

Browse global pins, and build themed collections — coffee, hikes, photo spots, and more.
